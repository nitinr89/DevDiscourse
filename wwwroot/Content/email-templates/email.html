Mother, Not Patient!
 

There are various researches to suggest that positive thinking and pleasant ambiance are boons for well-being of pregnant women and holistic development of the fetus while negative thinking and tension cause adverse effects.  
 

Deteriorating ambiance of positive thinking due to urbanization, nuclear families, modern lifestyle, increasing anonymity, and greed in a section of health and public health professionals adversely affect the health and well-being of the pregnant women.

 

The sufferings of pregnant women start from the day they are registered as ‘patient’ in hospitals as thereafter they are treated as patients till delivery of the child.
